# Notifications

## What is a Notification?

Notifications are messages sent to an [account user](users.md) via a supported channel: **push, email, SMS, voice, WhatsApp, or Slack**.

They are generated by [alerts](alerts.md), [broadcasts](broadcasts.md), and the PagerTree system. Notifications **cannot** be manually created.

---

PagerTree sends SMS and voice notifications from the following numbers:

- +1 (817) 592-8301
- +1 (817) 539-6888
- +1 (817) 592-6665

:::tip
Download the [PagerTree contact card (vCard)](https://pagertree.com/assets/img/kb/notifications/pagertree_vcard.vcf) to save these numbers to your phone as a single contact. This is also required for DnD bypass — see [Bypass Do Not Disturb](#bypass-do-not-disturb).
:::

:::note
Voice notifications are only sent for **alert open** events. You will not receive voice notifications for alert timeouts, drops, broadcasts, or other system events.
:::

## Bypass Do Not Disturb

There are two methods to bypass DnD — setting up the **PagerTree contact** and configuring the **PagerTree app**. We recommend doing **both** for the most reliable alerting.

First, download and save the [PagerTree contact card (vCard)](https://pagertree.com/assets/img/kb/notifications/pagertree_vcard.vcf), then follow the steps for your device below.

### Bypass DnD — Android

There are two complementary methods to bypass DnD on Android. We recommend configuring **both** for the most reliable alerting - one covers push notifications via the app, and the other covers calls and SMS via your contacts.

#### Method 1: Allow PagerTree App to Bypass DnD

This ensures push notifications from PagerTree can always interrupt DnD.

1. Open **Settings** and tap **Modes**.

<figure class="figure-mobile">![android modes](<.gitbook/assets/android-bypass-dnd-modes.jpeg>)</figure>

2. Tap **Do Not Disturb**.

<figure class="figure-mobile">![android do not disturb mode](<.gitbook/assets/android-bypass-dnd-modes-do-not-disturb.jpeg>)<figcaption><p>Tap "Do Not Disturb" to configure its exceptions</p></figcaption></figure>

3. Under **Notification filters**, tap **Apps**.

4. Make sure **Selected apps** is chosen, then tap the **gear icon** next to it.

<figure class="figure-mobile">![apps bypass setting](<.gitbook/assets/android-bypass-dnd-apps-bypass.jpeg>)</figure>

5. Confirm **PagerTree** appears in the list of apps that can interrupt. If it is not listed, tap **Add apps** and add it.

<figure class="figure-mobile">![pagertree allowed apps](<.gitbook/assets/android-bypass-dnd-allowed-apps.jpeg>)</figure>

#### Method 2: Allow Starred Contacts to Bypass DnD

This ensures PagerTree voice calls and SMS messages can always interrupt DnD.

**First, star the PagerTree contact:**

1. Open the **Contacts** app and tap the **PagerTree** contact.
2. Tap the **Star** icon in the top right to mark it as a favorite.

<figure class="figure-mobile">![star pagertree contact](<.gitbook/assets/android-bypass-dnd-starred-contact.jpeg>)</figure>

**Then, allow starred contacts through DnD:**

1. Open **Settings** and tap **Modes** → **Do Not Disturb**.
2. Under **Notification filters**, tap **People**.

<figure class="figure-mobile">![people bypass](<.gitbook/assets/android-bypass-dnd-people-bypass.jpeg>)</figure>

3. Tap **Calls**, then select **Starred contacts**.

<figure class="figure-mobile">![calls bypass starred contacts](<.gitbook/assets/android-bypass-dnd-calls-bypass.jpeg>)</figure>

4. Go back and tap **Messages**, then check **Starred contacts**.

<figure class="figure-mobile">![messages bypass starred contacts](<.gitbook/assets/android-bypass-dnd-messages-bypass.jpeg>)</figure>

#### Method 3: Enable Override DnD in the PagerTree App

:::info
The alert urgency must be set to **`critical`** for this to take effect.
:::

1. From the app home screen, tap [**My Settings**](https://app.pagertree.com/user/settings).

<figure>![](<.gitbook/assets/android-select-my-settings.png>)</figure>

2. Scroll to **Other Settings** and tap **Mobile App Settings**.

<figure>![](<.gitbook/assets/android-select-open-mobile-app-settings.png>)</figure>

3. In Android Settings:
   1. Tap **Notifications**.

   <figure>![](<.gitbook/assets/android-select-app-notifications.png>)</figure>

   2. Tap **Critical**.
   3. Enable **Override Do Not Disturb**.

   <figure>![](<.gitbook/assets/android-select-override-dnd.png>)</figure>

All `critical` urgency alerts will now trigger a Critical Alert on your iPhone, bypassing DnD and playing a loud audible sound.

## Notification Persistence (Push)

When persistent notifications are enabled, push notifications are re-sent every **90 seconds** for up to **1 hour** if unacknowledged.

To enable:

1. Navigate to [My Settings](https://app.pagertree.com/user/settings).
2. Scroll to **Other Settings**.
3. Select the alert urgency level that should use persistent notifications.

<figure>![persistent notification setting](<.gitbook/assets/select-persistent-notification-setting.png>)<figcaption><p>Persistent Notification Setting</p></figcaption></figure>

4. Click **Update**.

## Troubleshooting Push Notifications

If you are not receiving push notifications, work through the following steps in order:

1. **Check user preferences** — Ensure push notifications are enabled in your [user preferences](users.md#default-notification-channels).

<figure>![Push Notifications Enabled](<.gitbook/assets/push-notifications-enabled.png>)<figcaption><p>Push Notifications Enabled</p></figcaption></figure>

2. **Check app permissions** — Ensure the PagerTree app has notification permissions enabled in your phone settings.

<figure>![android permissions](<.gitbook/assets/android-permissions.png>)<figcaption><p>Android Permissions</p></figcaption></figure>

3. **Check device registration** — Ensure your [device is registered](#register-your-device).

4. **Check notification rules** — Ensure no [notification rules](notification-rules.md#attach-the-notification-rules-to-your-user) are overriding push notifications.

### Reset Push Notification Token

If you're still not receiving notifications after the steps above, try resetting your push token:

1. Navigate to [My Settings](https://app.pagertree.com/user/settings).
2. Scroll to **App Registrations**.
3. Click **Remove** on all registered devices.
4. Click **Update** to save.
5. Click **Register this device** to re-register.
6. Click **Send Test Notification** to verify.

### Reinstall the App

If resetting the token doesn't resolve the issue, reinstalling the app may help.

**Before reinstalling:**
1. Navigate to [My Settings](https://app.pagertree.com/user/settings).
2. Scroll to **App Registrations** and click **Remove** on all devices.
3. Click **Update** to save.

**Reinstall:**
1. Uninstall the PagerTree app from your device.
2. Reinstall from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.pagertree.app&pcampaignid=MKT-Other-global-all-co-prtnr-py-PartBadge-Mar2515-1) (Android) or [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/pagertree/id1266437807) (iOS).

**After reinstalling:**
1. Open the app and allow all permissions when prompted.
2. Navigate to [My Settings](https://app.pagertree.com/user/settings).
3. Click **Register this device**.
4. Send a test notification to confirm everything is working.

## Troubleshooting WhatsApp Notifications

If you are not receiving WhatsApp notifications:

1. Ensure WhatsApp notifications are enabled in your user preferences.
2. Ensure no [notification rules](notification-rules.md#attach-the-notification-rules-to-your-user) are overriding WhatsApp notifications.

If notifications still aren't arriving or show an **undelivered** status, open WhatsApp and send a message with the content `?` to the PagerTree WhatsApp number: **+1 (817) 592-8301**. This re-establishes the connection between PagerTree and WhatsApp, which can sometimes be interrupted if messages are flagged as spam.
